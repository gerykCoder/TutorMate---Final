Guidelines for Tutees

Selecting a Tutor and Scheduling a Tutorial

  • Before registering for a tutorial, visit the "List of Tutors" section to find tutors teaching your desired course.
  • Review the tutors' time availability. Time frames with check marks indicate available slots.
  • Once you have identified a suitable tutor and time slot, proceed to fill out the tutorial registration form with the required details, such as the subject, topic, and preferred time.
  • The minimum duration for a tutorial session is 1 hour.
  • Tutorial sessions are scheduled within the time range of 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM.
  • Tutorials will only be held onsite, from Monday to Saturday.

Tutorial Status and Cancelation Rules

  • Pending Tutorials: These are tutorial requests awaiting tutor approval. You are free to cancel pending tutorials without penalty.
  • Scheduled Tutorials: These are tutorials that have been accepted by the tutor. Canceling scheduled tutorials will result in a penalty.
  • Upon reaching three penalties, your account will be banned from using the tutorial management system.

Attending Tutorials

  • Be on time and prepared for your scheduled tutorial session.
  • If you have no communication with your tutor regarding the meeting place, proceed infront of Q-9211 (faculty room) and wait for your tutor there.
  • Respect your tutor and actively participate during the session for the best learning experience.

General Etiquette

  • Use the system responsibly by avoiding duplicate or unnecessary tutorial requests.
  • Maintain professionalism in all interactions with tutors and administrators.
